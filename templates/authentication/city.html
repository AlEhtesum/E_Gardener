City Garden

Garden Ideas:

  • Water Features: A sleek water feature like a koi pond or a simple water wall can bring tranquility and a dynamic visual element.
  • Vertical Gardening: Utilize your vertical space by adding climbing plants or installing green walls. This is particularly effective in smaller gardens.
  • Integrated Seating: Include built-in seating within your garden, such as bench seats built into retaining walls or around fire pits.
  • Outdoor Lighting: Use modern lighting fixtures to highlight architectural plants, illuminate pathways, and create a welcoming atmosphere for evening entertainment.
  • Contrasting Textures: Incorporate gravel, concrete, wood, and other materials to add layers of texture and depth.
  • Art Pieces: Consider integrating modern art pieces or sculptures as focal points within your garden.
