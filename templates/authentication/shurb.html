Flowering Shurb

Box wood

Boxwood is an evergreen shrub known for its dense, green foliage and ability to be shaped into various forms. It is often used in formal garden settings and as hedging due to its compact growth habit. Boxwood requires well-drained soil and partial to full sun to thrive.

Camellia

Camellia shrubs are prized for their stunning, rose-like flowers that bloom in late winter to spring. They have glossy, dark green leaves that provide year-round interest in the garden. Camellias prefer acidic, well-drained soil and dappled shade.

Forsythia

Forsythia is known for its bright yellow flowers that herald the arrival of spring. This fast-growing shrub is easy to care for and can be used for hedging or as a standalone feature. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Lilac

Lilac shrubs are celebrated for their fragrant, colorful flowers that bloom in late spring. They are available in a range of colors, including purple, white, and pink. Lilacs prefer full sun and well-drained soil for optimal growth and flowering

Hydrangea

Hydrangeas are beloved for their large, showy flower heads that come in various colors, often changing with soil pH. They can be used as focal points in the garden or planted in groups for a dramatic effect. These shrubs prefer moist, well-drained soil and can tolerate both sun and shade.

Rhododendron

Rhododendrons are known for their large clusters of colorful flowers and glossy green leaves. They are ideal for woodland gardens and shaded areas. These shrubs thrive in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Mock Orange

Mock Orange is famous for its citrus-scented white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is a versatile shrub that can be used as a hedge, in borders, or as a standalone plant. Mock Orange prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Butterfly Bush

The Butterfly Bush is known for attracting butterflies with its long, cone-shaped flower clusters. It blooms from summer to fall and is available in various colors, including purple, pink, and white. This shrub thrives in full sun and well-drained soil
